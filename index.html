Rizal’s Education from Elementary to College

by Manankil,Monteclaro,Mallari July 11,2024

1861-1872

Jose Rizal's education started all the way to his mother, Rizal's first instructor was his own mother Teodora Alonso Realonda; she taught him to read, and pray, and even motivated him to compose poetry. during his early years he was also later taught Spanish and Latin by his private tutors. He was also assigned to a private school in Binan which was the start of his formal education.

Ang kabataan ang pag-asa ng bayan.

1872-1877

At this time Rizal began his secondary schooling at the Ateneo Municipal De Manila. He took philosophy,physics,chemistry, and natural history, he also received top grades in his secondary years. He read books and composed poetry that made him win awards. While in his secondary years he developed longtime hobbies such as drawing, painting, and clay modeling.

Ang pag-ibig sa bayan ay hindi nasusukat sa oras ng kalamidad, kundi sa araw-araw na pagtuturing at pagtupad sa ating mga tungkulin.

1877-1882

Rizal eventually graduated from Ateneo Municipal with a degree in land surveying and appriasal while studying philosophy and letters at the University of Santo tomas, which he took medicine as his course. At this period of time he went to europe to study for Licentiate in Philosophy and Letters and Opthamoloy at the Universidad Central de Madrid

Kanya-kanya ng kaalaman ang tao. Ang talino at kakayahan ng tao ay hindi sa lahat ng bagay. Samakatuwid, pakialaman lamang natin yung mga bagay na mayroon tayong kaalaman.

1882-1884

After rizal finished his studies in hid medical course, he started to travel around europe and asia. The very first country he visited at that time was spain followed by singapore,ceylon, italy, france, barcelona in spain, paris, madrid and so many more.

Nalilimot ng bawat isa sa inyo na habang napag-iingatan ang isang bayan ang kaniyang wika, napag-iingatan din nito ang katibayan ng kaniyang paglaya, katulad ng pagpapanatili ng isang tao sa kaniyang kasarinlan, upang mapanatili niya ang kaniyang sariling paraan ng pag-iisip. Ang wika ang pag-iisip ng bayan.

1884-1887
